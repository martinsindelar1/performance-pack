The big five · per bucket

● Positives

  • Klaviyo flows + campaigns drove 12.7M CZK — 39.8% of total revenue. April flows alone +44% vs Jan.
  • Revenue grew every month Jan→Apr (+8.9% cumulative) despite traffic volatility.
  • Conversion rate jumped to 2.75% in April from 1.7–1.9% baseline. AOV stable at 1.28K CZK.
  • Net profit up 19% Jan→Apr despite ad spend +13% — operational discipline holding.
  • TikTok ROAS spike to 12.77 in April (from 2.10 in Mar). Worth investigating.

● Negatives

  • New Customer CPA up +30% Jan→Apr (623→813 CZK). The #1 red flag.
  • Blended ROAS sliding 5.45→5.25 (−3.7%). Ad spend growing faster than ad revenue.
  • Behind FY26 budget by 28% YTD — 31.96M actual vs 44.66M planned for Jan–Apr.
  • Meta CPC up +10% (7.96→8.73 CZK), Meta CTR down to 0.71% in April from 0.96% in March.
  • Acquisition shrinking: new customers down 440→350 (−20%) while spend up.

● Opportunities

  • Endurance Season (Apr 12–15, 2+1) coincides with April CR spike to 2.75%. Replicate mechanic for Summer prep.
  • Klaviyo headroom: Campaigns scaled +44%, Flows flat. Flow optimization is next unlock.
  • Google ROAS 18–22× — push more spend; currently only 19% of ad budget.
  • TikTok efficiency improving while spend dropping. Re-test creative-fund allocation.
  • Returning customer revenue +26% (4.71M→5.96M) — loyalty engine working. Push referral + Pro program harder.

Data quality notes

Read before reacting

Caveats & open questions

  • Single-source pack: all data from Triple Whale export. Shopify, Klaviyo direct, and UppPromote not yet reconciled — cross-source audit is Action #5.
  • Czech locale formatting: TW export uses dots as thousand separators (99.658 = 99,658 users; 0.44 = 440 new customers). I normalized everything to integer counts.
  • Excel date-conversion artifacts: some ratios got auto-converted to dates by Excel (e.g. "8/2" → Aug 2, 2026 for New Customer ROAS Jan). Best-effort restored: Jan New Customer ROAS = 8.2, Mar Google CTR ≈ 2.5–3% (estimated as 7.3% would be unreasonably high), Mar TikTok ROAS = 2.10, Mar TikTok CPC = 1.6 (verify against TT Ads Manager).
  • Conversion rate definition: TW reports against Sessions. April 2.75% is unusually high — confirm with Shopify analytics (typical Complex CR is 1.7–2.0%).
  • Budget benchmark: FY26 plan per finance file: Jan 7.83M + Feb 9.96M + Mar 13.31M + Apr 13.55M = 44.66M GS planned vs 31.96M actual. Note this is Gross Sales (incl. VAT?) vs the TW "Order Revenue" figure — need clarification on definitions before locking the −28% miss number.
  • New Customer Revenue + Returning Customer Revenue ≠ Order Revenue exactly (rounding + Other category). Variance <0.5% so used directly.
